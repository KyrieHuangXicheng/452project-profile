Customer Support Process

  1. Customer encountering problems: Customers encounter any questions or difficulties during the use of products or services
  2. Access Support Page: Customers first visit the company's support page, which includes resources such as FAQs, support documents, and video tutorials.
  3. Self service solution: Customers try to find a solution to the problem through FAQs, documentation, or tutorials.
  4. Submit support request: If the self-service solution is not successful, the customer can submit a support request, including problem description, relevant screenshots or files, and other information.
  5. Waiting for response: After the customer submits the request, wait for the company's support team to respond
  6. Support team intervention: After receiving the request, the support team analyzes the problem and provides a response or solution as soon as possible
